About Kashmir

Kashmir, known as "Paradise on Earth," is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. Nestled in the Himalayas, it is home to snow-covered peaks, serene lakes, and lush valleys.

Seasons in Kashmir

  • Spring: Almond and cherry blossoms paint the valley pink and white.
  • Summer: Green meadows and flowing rivers welcome travelers.
  • Autumn: Golden chinar leaves blanket the ground.
  • Winter: Snow turns the valley into a wonderland.

Kashmiri Cuisine

Food is a big part of Kashmiri culture. Famous dishes include:

  • Rogan Josh – A flavorful lamb curry.
  • Gushtaba – Minced meatballs cooked in yogurt gravy.
  • Kahwa – Traditional saffron-flavored green tea with almonds.

Traditional Crafts

Kashmiri artisans are known for exquisite handicrafts such as:

  • Pashmina shawls
  • Hand-knotted carpets
  • Wood carvings and papier-mâché
